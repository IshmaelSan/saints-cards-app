- Our Mission -

We are proposing a web application that will assist Santa Fe College students with studying for exams and quizzes. The main purpose of this application is to improve performance in courses by creating virtual flashcards. These flashcards can be created by the student but the focus of our flashcards will be official pre-made flashcards by the instructor.
